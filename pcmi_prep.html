The details:

This is a webpage for a series of lectures organized in preparation for the 2019 PCMI Graduate Summer School, with the aim of covering some of the prerequisites to some of the courses there. Unless otherwise specified, talks will be in PMA 8.136 at 11 AM. These lectures are organized by Charlie Reid.

  2. Witten-Reshetikhin-Turaev invariants I, Charlie Reid, May 22

  3. Khovanov homology, Will Stewart, May 23

  4. Witten-Reshetikhin-Turaev invariants II, Charlie Reid, May 24

  5. Bordism and Thom spectra, Arun Debray, June 4

  6. Bordism categories and invertible field theories, Arun Debray, June 6

  7. Complex geometry, Ali Shehper, June 11
