Quantum Topology and Categorification

Spring 2017 Learning Seminar

This is the webpage for a learning seminar on quantum physics and categorification in low-dimensional topology. In Spring 2017, we're planning to work through two extended examples: Witten's derivation of the Jones polynomial from Chern-Simons theory and Khovanov's categorification of the Jones polynomial into Khovanov homology. The seminar is organized by Arun Debray and Richard Wong.

Logistics: we'll meet Tuesdays at 2:00 PM in room RLM 11.176. We'll tackle the Jones polynomial and Chern-Simons theory in the first half of the semester, then move to Khovanov homology.
